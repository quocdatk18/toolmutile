================================================================================

PROJECT: Fix Jun88 bot detection when auto form + manual captcha
STATUS: ✅ COMPLETE & READY FOR TESTING
DATE: 2025-12-18

================================================================================
PROBLEM
================================================================================
- Form được điền xong, tool click đăng kí
- User tự giải captcha
- Trang báo "Đăng kí không thành công" - phát hiện bot

ROOT CAUSE:
- Form được điền quá nhanh (< 1s)
- Không có delay giữa các tương tác
- Trang phát hiện automation patterns

================================================================================
SOLUTION
================================================================================
1. Slow Form Filling (15-20s)
   - 150ms per character (was instant)
   - 300ms before focus, 800ms after fill
   - Delays between each field

2. Increased Delays (8-25s)
   - Random delay before submit (was 5-20s)
   - Scroll simulation (down + up)
   - Slow button click (200ms + 100ms)

3. Anti-bot Measures
   - Scroll page before submit
   - Hover before click
   - Detailed logging
   - Category detection (JUN88 specific)
